About OpenDike

OpenDike is an open-source project designed to assist students and professionals with various hydraulic problems. It excels in calculating the resistance of dikes in abstract scenarios for exams or in real-world projects where miscalculations could potentially lead to the flooding of areas protected by the dike.

Our software helps you navigate the complexities of water management and dike engineering with precision and ease, ensuring the safety and reliability of hydraulic structures.
